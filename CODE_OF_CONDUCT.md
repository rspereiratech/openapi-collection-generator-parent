# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions when uncertain.
- Focus discussion on technical merit and project goals.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Credit others' work and ideas.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Off-topic or disruptive contributions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with the project.

## Reporting

If you observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it privately to the maintainer at **rspereiratech@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source community guidelines, including the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
